Skill Match

Skill Match is a web app seeking to help local governments organize and optimize sports meet-ups. Every year, billions are spent by Parks and Recreation in the United States to keep local citizens involved and active. In Henderson alone, the 2016 budget for technical services is $340,000. Skill Match gives the government an easy to use platform for users to find and create local sports meet-ups. Anonymous feedback helps rank games and players, so meet-ups can be more efficiently organized, offering a better experience for everyone involved. You can see our story --> HERE.
